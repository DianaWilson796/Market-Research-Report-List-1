<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li><li>Universal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3-axis CNC milling machine market includes horizontal, vertical, and universal types. Horizontal machines have a spindle parallel to the worktable, ideal for heavy-duty and deep-cutting tasks. Vertical machines feature a spindle perpendicular to the table, suitable for precision work and smaller parts. Universal machines combine both horizontal and vertical capabilities, offering flexibility for complex geometries and multi-angle machining. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing precision, versatility, and efficiency in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
