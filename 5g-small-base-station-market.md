<p><p>The 5G small base station market consists primarily of two types: Stand Alone (SA) and Non-Stand Alone (NSA). SA architecture operates independently on 5G core networks, offering full benefits of 5G technology like ultra-low latency and high capacity. NSA, in contrast, relies on existing 4G infrastructure for control functions, facilitating a quicker transition to 5G while leveraging current investments. Both types cater to diverse deployment needs, enhancing coverage and capacity in urban and remote areas while supporting various applications.</p></p>
