<p><ul><li>Convex Array</li><li>Concave Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The achromatic lens element market includes two main types: convex arrays and concave arrays. Convex arrays are designed to converge light, correcting chromatic aberrations while enhancing focus and clarity, making them ideal for applications like cameras and telescopes. Conversely, concave arrays diverge light, providing a wider field of view and minimizing distortion, which is beneficial in systems requiring broad light capture. Both types play a crucial role in optical systems, catering to various technological and imaging needs.</p></p>
