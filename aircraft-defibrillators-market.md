<p><p>The aircraft defibrillators market comprises three primary types: Automatic, Manual, and Semi-Automatic. Automatic defibrillators deliver shocks without user intervention, ideal for quick response during emergencies. Manual defibrillators require trained personnel to control shock delivery, offering greater precision but demanding expertise. Semi-Automatic defibrillators guide users through the process, combining ease of use with some level of control. These types cater to varying operational needs in aviation, ensuring effective cardiac care in-flight with emphasis on response time and user capability.</p></p>
