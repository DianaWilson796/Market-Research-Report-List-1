<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aircraft Tire Retreading Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Cure</li><li>Mold-Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft tire retreading market consists of two main types: pre-cure and mold-cure. Pre-cure retreading involves applying a pre-formed tread to a prepared tire casing, allowing for flexible tread designs and quick processing. In contrast, mold-cure retreading utilizes a mold to shape and cure the new tread directly onto the tire, ensuring a uniform and consistent finish. Both methods aim to extend the lifespan of aircraft tires, enhance performance, and provide cost-effective solutions for airlines and operators.</p></p>
