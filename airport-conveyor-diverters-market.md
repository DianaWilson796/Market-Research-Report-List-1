<p><p>The airport conveyor diverters market is categorized into three main types: horizontal, vertical, and modular systems. Horizontal diverters facilitate the lateral movement of baggage, ensuring efficient sorting and directing along the conveyor belts. Vertical diverters, on the other hand, enable the upward or downward transfer of bags between different conveyor levels, optimizing space and workflow. Modular diverters provide customizable configurations, allowing airports to adapt the system according to layout and operational needs, enhancing overall efficiency in baggage handling.</p></p>
