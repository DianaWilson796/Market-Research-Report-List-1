Pipeline mixers integrate directly into hospital gas supply systems, ensuring precise gas mixtures at high flow rates. Static mixers, on the other hand, rely on a series of fixed elements to blend gases without moving parts, providing consistent mixtures with lower maintenance needs. The "other" category encompasses specialized or emerging technologies that offer unique features or applications in anesthetic gas delivery.</p></p>
