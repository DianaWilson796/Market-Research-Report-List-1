<p><p>The anti-biofilm wound dressing market encompasses three primary types: physical, chemical, and biological. Physical dressings focus on creating a barrier that prevents biofilm formation through materials that hinder bacterial adhesion. Chemical dressings utilize antimicrobial agents that actively disrupt biofilm structure or bacterial growth. Biological dressings incorporate natural substances, such as enzymes or peptides, that target and degrade biofilms while promoting healing. Together, these approaches aim to improve wound healing outcomes by effectively managing biofilm-related challenges.</p></p>
