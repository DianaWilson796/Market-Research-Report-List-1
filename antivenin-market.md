<p><strong>The Antivenin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyvalent</li><li>Monovalent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antivenin market is primarily divided into two types: polyvalent and monovalent antivenins. Polyvalent antivenins are designed to neutralize venom from multiple species of snakes or other venomous animals, making them useful in regions with diverse snake populations. In contrast, monovalent antivenins target the venom of a specific species, offering a focused treatment option. The choice between these types depends on factors such as geographical distribution of venomous species and the prevalence of snakebite incidents in the area.</p></p>
