<p><p>The artificial tears market is segmented into two main types: artificial tear liquids and artificial tear ointments. Artificial tear liquids are thin, watery solutions designed to mimic natural tears, providing immediate relief from dryness and irritation. They are typically available in preservative-free formulations for sensitive eyes. In contrast, artificial tear ointments are thicker and provide longer-lasting moisture, making them ideal for overnight use or for more severe dry eye conditions. Both types aim to improve comfort and eye health.</p></p>
