<p><p>The audio baby monitors market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless audio baby monitors. In contrast, wireless audio baby monitors utilize radio frequency or Wi-Fi technology, offering portability and flexibility. Wireless models are increasingly popular due to their convenience, allowing parents to monitor their baby from various locations within the home.</p></p>
