<p><p>Automated Parking Management Systems can be categorized into two primary types: Semi-Automated Parking Systems and Fully Automated Parking Systems. Semi-Automated Parking Systems require some driver involvement, where users park their vehicles in designated areas, and the system maneuvers them to parking spots. In contrast, Fully Automated Parking Systems operate with complete automation; vehicles are driven into an entry point, and the system handles all aspects of parking, retrieval, and storage without driver intervention, enhancing efficiency and optimizing space utilization.</p></p>
