<p><p>Automated Tank Gauges (ATG) enhance inventory management in various industries. Float-operated tank gauges use a buoyant float to measure liquid levels, while servo-operated gauges utilize a sophisticated system of a float and a servo motor for precise readings. Radar tank gauges employ microwave radar technology for non-contact measurement. Hydrostatic tank gauges rely on pressure sensors to determine liquid levels based on pressure differences. Other types may include ultrasonic or capacitance gauges, catering to specific applications and liquid types.</p></p>
