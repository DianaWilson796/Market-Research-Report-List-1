<p><p>The Automatic Elemental Analyzer market encompasses various types of chromatography techniques. Gas Chromatography (GC) separates and analyzes volatile compounds based on their vaporization properties. Frontal Chromatography focuses on the continuous introduction of a sample, allowing for direct measurement of component interactions with the stationary phase. Adsorption-Desorption methods involve capturing analytes on a stationary phase and subsequently releasing them for analysis, facilitating the study of material composition. Together, these techniques enhance the precision and efficiency of elemental analysis across various applications.</p></p>
