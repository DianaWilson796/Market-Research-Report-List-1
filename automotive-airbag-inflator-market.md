<p><strong>The Automotive Airbag Inflator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The automotive airbag inflator market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Pyrotechnic Gas Inflator**: These use a chemical reaction to rapidly produce gas, inflating the airbag upon impact.</p><p>2. **Hybrid Gas Inflator**: Combining pyrotechnic and stored gas methods, hybrid inflators utilize gas from a chemical reaction and pressurized gas, allowing for more controlled inflation.</p><p>3. **Stored Gas Inflator**: These rely solely on compressed gas, ensuring rapid deployment without a chemical reaction, offering a more straightforward and reliable inflation process.</p></p>
