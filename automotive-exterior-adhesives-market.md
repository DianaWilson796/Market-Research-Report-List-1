<p><ul><li>One-Component</li><li>Two-Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive exterior adhesives market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready to use, requiring no mixing, making them convenient for applications where quick bonding is essential. In contrast, two-component adhesives require a combination of resins and hardeners, offering superior bonding strength and durability, ideal for critical automotive parts exposed to harsh conditions. Both types cater to diverse bonding needs in vehicle assembly, repair, and customization.</p></p>
