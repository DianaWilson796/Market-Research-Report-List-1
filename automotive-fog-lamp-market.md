Rising consumer awareness about road safety and the growing adoption of luxury and premium vehicles equipped with advanced lighting systems are key factors contributing to this growth.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include the integration of LED technology, which offers higher energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and better illumination compared to traditional halogen lamps. <p><p>The Automotive Fog Lamp Market includes Front Fog Lights and Rear Fog Lights, designed to enhance visibility and safety in adverse weather conditions. Front Fog Lights are mounted low on the vehicle's front, emitting a wide, flat beam to cut through fog, rain, or snow, improving road visibility. Rear Fog Lights, positioned at the back, emit a bright red light to make the vehicle more visible to following drivers, reducing rear-end collision risks. Both types are essential for safe driving in low-visibility environments.</p></p>
