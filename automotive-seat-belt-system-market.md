<p><p>The automotive seat belt system market is divided into two main types: active and passive seat belt systems. Active seat belt systems enhance safety by automatically adjusting tension and positioning during a collision or sudden stop, often integrating features like pre-tensioning and load limiting. In contrast, passive seat belt systems provide basic restraint functionality, ensuring occupants are secured in their seats but without additional active features. Both systems are essential for improving occupant safety and are influenced by advancements in automotive technology.</p></p>
