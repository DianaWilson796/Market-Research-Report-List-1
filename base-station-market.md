<p><p>In the base station market, single-band and multi-band types refer to the capability to operate on different frequency bands. Single-band base stations support only one specific frequency, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for dedicated networks. In contrast, multi-band base stations can operate on multiple frequency bands simultaneously, providing greater flexibility, improved capacity, and better coverage in diverse environments. This versatility is particularly valuable for operators looking to enhance service quality and accommodate various technologies and user demands.</p></p>
