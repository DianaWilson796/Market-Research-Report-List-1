<p><p>The benchtop refrigerated centrifuge market is categorized into high-speed and low-speed centrifuges. High-speed benchtop refrigerated centrifuges operate at high RPMs, enabling efficient separation of biological samples, proteins, and nanoparticles, making them ideal for research laboratories. In contrast, low-speed benchtop refrigerated centrifuges function at lower RPMs, suitable for applications like blood sample processing and cell culture, where gentle separation is required to preserve sample integrity. Both types play essential roles in various scientific and clinical applications.</p></p>
