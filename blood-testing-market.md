<p><p>The blood testing market includes various types of tests that assess different health indicators. Glucose tests measure blood sugar levels, crucial for diabetes management. Lipid panels evaluate cholesterol levels to assess cardiovascular risk. BUN (Blood Urea Nitrogen) tests assess kidney function. A1C tests provide average blood sugar levels over time. CRP (C-Reactive Protein) indicates inflammation. Vitamin D tests assess bone health and immune function. ALT and AST are liver enzyme tests that indicate liver health, while Thyroid Stimulating Hormone (TSH) tests evaluate thyroid function.</p></p>
