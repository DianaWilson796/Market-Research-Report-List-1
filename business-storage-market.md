<p><p>The business storage market comprises two main types: non-climate controlled storage and climate-controlled storage. Non-climate controlled storage offers basic protection for items that are resilient to environmental changes, making it a cost-effective option for businesses with durable goods. In contrast, climate-controlled storage maintains specific temperature and humidity levels, protecting sensitive items such as electronics, documents, or perishables. Each type caters to distinct business needs, balancing cost, safety, and item preservation requirements effectively.</p></p>
