<p><p>The cabinet drawer runners market is categorized into three main types: wooden runners, roller runners, and ball bearing runners. Wooden runners offer a traditional aesthetic and natural feel but may lack smoothness over time. Roller runners use rollers to facilitate easy sliding, providing decent durability and affordability. In contrast, ball bearing runners utilize ball bearings for enhanced smoothness and strength, allowing for heavier loads and longevity. Each type caters to different preferences and functional needs in cabinetry design.</p></p>
