<p><ul><li>Caustic Calcined (Light Burned)</li><li>Heavy Burned</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calcined magnesium oxide market is primarily divided into two types: caustic calcined (light burned) and heavy burned magnesium oxide. Caustic calcined, also known as light burned magnesium oxide, is produced at lower temperatures and has a higher surface area, making it suitable for applications like pharmaceuticals and food additives. In contrast, heavy burned magnesium oxide, produced at higher temperatures, has a denser structure and is used in industrial applications, such as refractory materials and steel manufacturing, due to its improved durability and performance.</p></p>
