<p><ul><li>2-Way Speakers</li><li>3-Way Speakers</li><li>4-Way Speakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car audio speakers are categorized into different types based on the number of speaker elements they contain. **2-way speakers** consist of a woofer for low frequencies and a tweeter for high frequencies, providing balanced sound for most users. **3-way speakers** add a midrange driver to enhance vocal clarity and detail. **4-way speakers** include an additional driver for further separation of sound frequencies, delivering an even richer and more dynamic audio experience. Each type caters to varying audio preferences and vehicle acoustics.</p></p>
