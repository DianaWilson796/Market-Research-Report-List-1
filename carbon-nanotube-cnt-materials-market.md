<p><p>The carbon nanotube (CNT) materials market primarily includes two types: single-walled carbon nanotubes (SWNTs) and multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs consist of a single cylindrical layer of carbon atoms, offering exceptional electrical, thermal, and mechanical properties, making them ideal for applications in electronics and nanocomposites. In contrast, MWNTs consist of multiple concentric layers, providing enhanced strength and structural stability, suitable for applications in materials reinforcement and energy storage devices. Both types are integral in advancing nanotechnology and materials science.</p></p>
