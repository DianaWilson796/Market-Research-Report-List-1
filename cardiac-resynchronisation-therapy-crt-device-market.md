<p><ul><li>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Pacemakers</li><li>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) device market comprises two primary types: CRT pacemakers and CRT defibrillators. CRT pacemakers are designed to improve heart function by synchronizing the contractions of the heart's ventricles, enhancing blood flow in heart failure patients. In contrast, CRT defibrillators combine the resynchronization capabilities with the ability to deliver shocks in case of life-threatening arrhythmias, providing both rhythm management and heart failure treatment. Together, these devices aim to improve patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
