<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cartilage Repair Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell-Based Therapy</li><li>Non-Cell Based Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cartilage repair market is primarily divided into two types: cell-based therapy and non-cell-based therapy. </p><p>Cell-based therapy involves the use of live cells, such as chondrocytes or stem cells, to regenerate damaged cartilage and restore joint function. This approach offers potential for personalized treatment and promotes natural healing.</p><p>Non-cell-based therapy, on the other hand, encompasses various methods that do not utilize live cells, such as scaffolds, biomaterials, and injectable hydrogels. These techniques focus on promoting cartilage repair through mechanical support and biochemical stimulation.</p></p>
