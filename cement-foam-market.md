<p><p>The cement foam market is categorized into liquid and solid types based on form and application. Liquid cement foam is a pre-mixed, flowable solution used for lightweight filling, insulation, and void stabilization. It is easy to apply and ideal for large-scale projects. Solid cement foam, often in block or panel form, is used for construction, thermal insulation, and soundproofing. It offers durability and structural strength, making it suitable for building walls, floors, and roofs. Both types cater to diverse construction needs, balancing lightweight properties with performance.</p></p>
