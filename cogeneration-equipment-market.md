<p><p>Cogeneration equipment encompasses various technologies for simultaneous production of electricity and useful thermal energy. Gas turbines are efficient for large-scale power generation, while steam turbines utilize steam from boilers to drive electricity generation. Electric generators convert mechanical energy into electrical power, and heat recovery steam generators (HRSG) capture waste heat for additional steam production. Boilers generate steam from water, and reciprocating engines provide reliable electricity through combustion, making them versatile for distributed energy systems in diverse applications.</p></p>
