<p><p>The commercial LED lighting driver market features several types tailored for different dimming and control needs. DALI (Digital Addressable Lighting Interface) allows for precise, programmable control. 0-10V dimming offers smooth adjustments for ambient lighting. Standard (non-dim) drivers are used where fixed brightness is sufficient. Triac dimming is popular for compatibility with existing systems using phase-cut technology. Smart drivers provide advanced features, including remote control and integration with IoT systems, enhancing energy efficiency and user convenience.</p></p>
