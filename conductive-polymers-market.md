<p><ul><li>Electrically Conducting Polymers</li><li>Thermally Conducting Polymers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductive polymers market includes electrically conducting and thermally conducting polymers. Electrically conducting polymers possess the ability to conduct electricity, making them ideal for applications in electronics, sensors, and anti-static materials. Thermally conducting polymers, on the other hand, efficiently transfer heat and are used in applications requiring thermal management, such as electronic packaging and heat sinks. Both types are increasingly important in advanced materials applications, driven by the rising demand for lightweight, efficient, and multifunctional materials in various industries.</p></p>
