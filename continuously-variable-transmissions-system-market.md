<p><p>The Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) system market includes two primary types: Metal Belt and Metal Chain. Metal Belt CVTs use a flexible metal belt running between two pulleys to provide seamless gear ratio changes, offering smooth acceleration and improved fuel efficiency. Metal Chain CVTs utilize a robust metal chain for higher torque capacity, making them suitable for heavier vehicles. Both systems enhance performance by eliminating fixed gear ratios, ensuring optimal engine efficiency across various driving conditions.</p></p>
