<p><ul><li>Shielded</li><li>Unshielded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper pigtail market is divided into two main types: shielded and unshielded. Shielded copper pigtails are designed to minimize electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for sensitive applications in telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, unshielded copper pigtails offer a simpler, cost-effective solution for general wiring needs where interference is less of a concern. Each type serves distinct purposes based on the level of protection required against external electrical noise and the application environment.</p></p>
