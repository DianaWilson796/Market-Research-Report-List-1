<p><p>The crimping tool market is categorized into manual crimping tools and hydraulic crimping tools. Manual crimping tools are typically handheld devices designed for smaller tasks that require less force, making them suitable for light-duty applications. In contrast, hydraulic crimping tools employ hydraulic power to facilitate high-pressure crimping, ideal for heavy-duty tasks and larger connectors. Each type serves distinct needs, with manual tools offering portability and ease, while hydraulic tools provide greater efficiency and strength for more demanding applications.</p></p>
