<p><p>Cylindrical type lithium-ion secondary batteries are categorized primarily into four types: 18650, 26650, 21700, and others. The 18650 battery, popular for its compact size and widespread use in laptops and electric vehicles, has a nominal voltage of 3.7V. The larger 26650 offers higher capacity and is suited for high-drain applications. The 21700 battery provides increased energy density and is gaining popularity in electric vehicles and energy storage. "Others" encompass various sizes and configurations tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
