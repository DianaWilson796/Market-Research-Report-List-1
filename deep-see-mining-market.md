<p><p>Deep seabed mining involves extracting valuable minerals from ocean floors, with three primary market types: Cobalt Rich Crusts (CRC), Manganese Nodules, and Seafloor Massive Sulphides (SMS). CRCs are rich in cobalt and rare earth elements, crucial for batteries and technology. Manganese Nodules contain manganese, nickel, and copper, forming on the seafloor, while SMS deposits are rich in metals like copper, gold, and silver, formed through hydrothermal processes. Each type presents unique extraction challenges and economic opportunities.</p></p>
