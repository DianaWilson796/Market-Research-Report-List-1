<p><strong>The Desensitization Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subcutaneous Immunotherapy</li><li>Sublingual Immunotherapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Desensitization market types include Subcutaneous Immunotherapy (SCIT) and Sublingual Immunotherapy (SLIT). SCIT involves administering allergens through injections to gradually build tolerance, often requiring regular clinic visits. In contrast, SLIT delivers allergens via tablets or drops under the tongue, offering a more convenient, self-administered option. Both methods aim to reduce allergic reactions by modulating the immune response, appealing to patients seeking effective long-term allergy relief. Growth in these markets is driven by rising allergy prevalence and advances in treatment options.</p></p>
