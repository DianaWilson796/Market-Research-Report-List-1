<p><strong>The Drive Shaft (Driveshaft) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Half Shaft</li><li>Propeller Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drive shaft market includes two primary types: **Half Shaft** and **Propeller Shaft**. A **Half Shaft** connects the differential to the wheels, transmitting power in front- or rear-wheel-drive vehicles. It is commonly used in independent suspension systems. A **Propeller Shaft** transfers torque from the transmission to the differential in rear-wheel or four-wheel-drive vehicles, often featuring a tubular design for durability. Both are critical components in automotive drivetrains, ensuring efficient power delivery and vehicle performance across various applications.</p></p>
