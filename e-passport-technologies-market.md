<p><strong>The E-passport Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biometrics</li><li>Radio-Frequency Identification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-passport technologies market primarily consists of two key types: biometrics and radio-frequency identification (RFID). Biometrics involves the use of unique physical characteristics, such as fingerprints or facial recognition, to enhance security and verify identity at border control. RFID, on the other hand, employs electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track a passport by embedding a microchip that stores essential data. Together, these technologies improve the efficiency, accuracy, and security of international travel and immigration processes.</p></p>
