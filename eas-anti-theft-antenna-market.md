<p><p>The EAS anti-theft antenna market is primarily segmented into three types: Radio Frequency (RF), Acousto-Magnetic (AM), and Electromagnetic Waves (EM). RF systems use electromagnetic fields to detect tagged items, making them popular for retail environments. AM systems combine magnetic and acoustic principles, offering enhanced detection capabilities and fewer false alarms. EM systems utilize electromagnetic fields for theft detection and are suited for various applications. Each type varies in function, efficiency, and deployment, catering to diverse security needs in retail and other sectors.</p></p>
