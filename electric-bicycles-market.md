<p><ul><li>Brush Electric Bicycle</li><li>Brushless Electric Bicycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric bicycle market comprises two main types: brush and brushless electric bicycles. Brush electric bicycles utilize brushed motors, which include brushes that create friction to generate power, typically resulting in more maintenance and reduced efficiency. In contrast, brushless electric bicycles employ brushless motors, offering greater efficiency, longer lifespan, and lower maintenance costs due to the absence of brushes. This distinction affects performance, durability, and overall user experience, catering to different segments within the growing e-bike market.</p></p>
