<p><p>The Electrical Impedance Tomography (EIT) device market is segmented into Static and Dynamic EIT types. Static EIT focuses on imaging the electrical properties of a given tissue or medium at a single moment, providing insights into its structure and composition. In contrast, Dynamic EIT emphasizes the temporal changes in electrical impedance over time, enabling real-time monitoring of physiological processes or flow dynamics. Both types contribute valuable data for medical diagnostics, biomedical research, and industrial applications.</p></p>
