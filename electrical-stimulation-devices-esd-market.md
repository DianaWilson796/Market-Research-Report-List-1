<p><p>Electrical Stimulation Devices (ESD) encompass various technologies designed to modulate nerve activity for therapeutic benefits. Spinal Cord Stimulation (SCS) devices alleviate chronic pain by sending electrical impulses to the spinal cord. Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) devices target neurological disorders by stimulating specific brain regions. Vagus Nerve Stimulation (VNS) devices treat epilepsy and depression through vagus nerve modulation. Sacral Nerve Stimulation (SNS) addresses bladder control issues, while Gastric Electrical Stimulation (GES) aids in obesity. Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) relieves pain externally, and Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation (NMES/EMS) promotes muscle recovery and strength. Other devices may include emerging therapies and applications.</p></p>
