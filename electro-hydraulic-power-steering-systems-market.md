<p><strong>The Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EHPS</li><li>P-EHPS</li><li>R-EHPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) systems combine hydraulic assistance with electric motor control for enhanced efficiency and performance. The market includes three main types: C-EHPS (Column-mounted EHPS), P-EHPS (Pinion-mounted EHPS), and R-EHPS (Rack-mounted EHPS). C-EHPS integrates the electric motor and hydraulic pump on the steering column, offering compact design and ease of installation. P-EHPS places the system on the pinion, balancing performance and space efficiency. R-EHPS mounts the system on the rack, providing superior steering precision and handling, ideal for high-performance vehicles. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements and driving dynamics.</p></p>
