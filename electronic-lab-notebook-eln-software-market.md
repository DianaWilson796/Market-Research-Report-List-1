<p><p>The Electronic Lab Notebook (ELN) software market can be categorized into cloud-based and web-based solutions. Cloud-based ELNs operate on remote servers, allowing users to access their data from any device with internet connectivity, ensuring data security and easy collaboration. Web-based ELNs, on the other hand, are accessed through web browsers, requiring no installation of software on local machines, thus simplifying deployment and maintenance. Both types enhance research documentation, increase efficiency, and facilitate real-time sharing among teams.</p></p>
