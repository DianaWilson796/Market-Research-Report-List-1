<p><p>The embolization particle market comprises various types tailored for different medical applications. Microspheres are tiny spherical particles utilized for precise vascular occlusion. Specific particles like polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) and Gelfoam are often used for their ability to block blood flow. Drug-eluting beads (DEBs) combine embolization with localized drug delivery, enhancing therapeutic effects. Radio-embolic microspheres, such as Therasphere and SIRSphere, emit radiation directly to tumors, improving cancer treatment outcomes while minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue.</p></p>
