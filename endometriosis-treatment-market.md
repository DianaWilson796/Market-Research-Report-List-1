<p><p>The endometriosis treatment market includes various approaches, such as hormonal contraceptives, progesterone replacement therapy, and GnRH analogues. Hormonal contraceptives, like birth control pills, help regulate menstruation and reduce pain. Progesterone replacement therapy aids in balancing hormones and alleviating symptoms. GnRH analogues suppress estrogen production, effectively reducing endometrial tissue but may induce temporary menopause-like symptoms. Each treatment type targets hormonal regulation to manage the condition, catering to different patient needs and stages of endometriosis.</p></p>
