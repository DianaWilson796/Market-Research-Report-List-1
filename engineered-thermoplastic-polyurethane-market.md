<p><ul><li>Foam</li><li>Elastomer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineered thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) market encompasses two primary types: foam and elastomer. Foam TPUs are lightweight, flexible, and offer excellent cushioning and impact resistance, making them ideal for applications in automotive seating, footwear, and protective equipment. Elastomer TPUs, on the other hand, are known for their superior abrasion resistance and elasticity, suitable for applications like automotive parts, seals, and gaskets. Both types provide versatility and durability, catering to diverse industries including automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods.</p></p>
