<p><ul><li>PVD</li><li>CVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The evaporation coating machine market primarily consists of two key types: Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD). PVD involves the physical transformation of materials into vapor, which then condenses on a substrate to form a thin film, commonly used for optical coatings and decorative finishes. In contrast, CVD utilizes chemical reactions to produce vapor that deposits onto surfaces, ideal for semiconductor manufacturing and coatings requiring superior uniformity. Both techniques cater to diverse industrial needs, driving market growth.</p></p>
