<p><p>The fabric filters market can be segmented into two main types: Pulse Jet and Reverse Air/Gas. Pulse Jet filters utilize a high-velocity burst of air to clean the fabric, effectively dislodging collected particles. This method is efficient for continuous operations where maintenance must be minimized. In contrast, Reverse Air/Gas filters operate by inverting the airflow to clean the fabric, which is ideal for applications with larger particulate loads. Both types serve crucial roles in industrial air pollution control.</p></p>
