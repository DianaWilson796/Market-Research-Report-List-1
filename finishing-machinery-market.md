<p><ul><li>Wet Technologies</li><li>Dry Technologies</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The finishing machinery market encompasses two main types: wet technologies and dry technologies. Wet technologies utilize liquid solutions to enhance the surface finish of materials, often involving processes like spraying, dipping, or chemical treatment. In contrast, dry technologies rely on mechanical or thermal methods to achieve finishing effects without the use of liquids, such as sanding, grinding, or polishing. Both technologies are crucial in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and textiles, to ensure high-quality surface characteristics and durability.</p></p>
