<p><p>The Flexible Fire Resistant Sealant market is primarily divided into two types: Elastomeric and Intumescent sealants. Elastomeric sealants are rubber-like materials that provide durable, flexible barriers against fire and smoke, maintaining their integrity under thermal stress. In contrast, Intumescent sealants expand when exposed to heat, forming a char layer that insulates and protects substrates from flames. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety in construction and industrial applications by preventing fire spread and enhancing overall fire resistance.</p></p>
