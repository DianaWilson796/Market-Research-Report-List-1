<p><strong>The Food Preservatives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antimicrobials</li><li>Antioxidants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food preservatives market comprises two main types: antimicrobials and antioxidants. Antimicrobials inhibit the growth of bacteria, molds, and yeasts, extending the shelf life of perishable foods and ensuring safety. Common examples include sodium benzoate and propionic acid. Antioxidants, on the other hand, prevent oxidation, which causes rancidity and color changes in fats and oils. Compounds such as ascorbic acid and tocopherols are widely used. Together, these preservatives play crucial roles in enhancing food quality and safety.</p></p>
