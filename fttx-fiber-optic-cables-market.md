<p><p>FTTx fiber optic cables encompass various deployment types that utilize fiber to connect homes or businesses. Single-mode fibers feature a small core diameter, allowing one light mode to travel, resulting in long-distance transmission with minimal loss, ideal for telecommunications. Conversely, multi-mode fibers have a larger core, enabling multiple light modes to propagate, making them suitable for shorter distances, such as within buildings or data centers. Both types are crucial for expanding broadband connectivity in modern networks.</p></p>
