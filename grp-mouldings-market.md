<p><strong>The GRP Mouldings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Special Wax</li><li>PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GRP (Glass Reinforced Plastic) mouldings market can be segmented into special wax and PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol) types, each serving distinct purposes. Special wax is used as a release agent, facilitating easy removal of moulds, enhancing surface finish, and providing a protective layer. PVA, on the other hand, acts as a film-forming agent and is crucial in the production of moulds, offering excellent release properties and water solubility. Both types contribute significantly to the efficiency and quality of GRP manufacturing processes.</p></p>
