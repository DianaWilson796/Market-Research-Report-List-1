<p><p>The guide wires market features three primary types: angled guide wires, J-shaped guide wires, and straight guide wires. Angled guide wires are designed for navigating complex anatomical pathways, offering flexibility and improved steerability. J-shaped guide wires facilitate entry into vessels with a gentle curvature, enhancing maneuverability in tight spaces. Straight guide wires are essential for straightforward access in procedures, providing reliable navigation and stability. These variations cater to diverse clinical needs, enhancing procedural efficiency and patient safety.</p></p>
