<p><p>The HPV testing market can be categorized into three types: HPV testing, follow-up HPV testing, and co-testing. HPV testing involves the initial screening for human papillomavirus to identify potential risks of cervical cancer. Follow-up HPV testing is conducted after abnormal results to monitor and assess the need for further interventions. Co-testing combines both HPV testing and Pap smears during cervical screening, enhancing accuracy and providing a comprehensive risk assessment for cervical cancer, leading to improved patient management.</p></p>
