<p><p>The hydraulic brake booster market is categorized into drum brake and disc brake types, each serving distinct applications. In contrast, disc brakes, favored for their superior performance, heat dissipation, and consistent stopping ability, are more prevalent in modern vehicles. The choice between these systems influences braking efficiency, maintenance, and overall vehicle safety.</p></p>
