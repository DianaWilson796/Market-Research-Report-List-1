<p><strong>The Industrial Centrifuges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sedimentation Centrifuges</li><li>Filtering Centrifuges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial centrifuge market is primarily divided into two types: sedimentation centrifuges and filtering centrifuges. Sedimentation centrifuges operate by utilizing centrifugal force to separate solids and liquids based on density differences. They are commonly used in waste treatment and chemical processing. Filtering centrifuges, on the other hand, use a porous medium to separate solid particles from liquid suspensions. These are widely employed in industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing, where the purity of the end product is critical.</p></p>
