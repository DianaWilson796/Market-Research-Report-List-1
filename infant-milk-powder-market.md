<p><p>The infant milk powder market comprises several types catering to specific nutritional needs. Cow's milk-based formula is the most common, providing essential nutrients similar to breast milk. Soy-based formula serves as an alternative for infants with lactose intolerance or dairy allergies. Protein hydrolysate-based formula contains pre-digested proteins for babies with severe allergies or digestion issues. Specialty formulas address specific conditions, such as prematurity or metabolic disorders, ensuring tailored nutrition for diverse infant health requirements.</p></p>
