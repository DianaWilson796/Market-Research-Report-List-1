<p><p>The intravenous immunoglobulins (IVIG) market comprises various immunoglobulin types: IgG, IgA, IgM, IgE, and IgD. IgG is the most prevalent, used primarily for immune deficiency and autoimmune diseases. IgA primarily protects mucosal surfaces, while IgM plays a crucial role in initial immune responses. IgE is involved in allergic reactions, and IgD is associated with B cell activation. Each type serves specific therapeutic purposes, influencing market dynamics and demand across diverse healthcare applications.</p></p>
