<p><p>The Inventory Tank Gauging (ITG) market comprises two primary types: electronic ITG and mechanical ITG. Electronic ITGs utilize advanced sensors and digital technology to provide precise measurements of liquid levels and other parameters, often featuring remote monitoring capabilities. Conversely, mechanical ITGs use traditional methods like float systems or pressure gauges to measure inventory levels, typically requiring manual readings. Both types serve critical functions in industries like oil and gas, ensuring accurate inventory management and compliance with safety regulations.</p></p>
