<p><strong>The Iron Ore Pelletizing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disc Pelletizers (Disk Granulators)</li><li>Rotary Drum Pelletizing Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The iron ore pelletizing equipment market primarily features two types: disc pelletizers and rotary drum pelletizers. Disc pelletizers, or disk granulators, use a rotating disk to agglomerate iron ore fines into pellets, allowing for precise control over pellet size and density. In contrast, rotary drum pelletizers utilize a cylindrical drum to mix iron ore with binders and moisture, promoting the formation of pellets through continuous rotation. Both types are essential in producing high-quality pellets for steel manufacturing.</p></p>
