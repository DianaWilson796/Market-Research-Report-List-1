<p><strong>The Kraft Paper Adhesive Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Rubber Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kraft paper adhesive tape is available in various types, primarily solvent-based and rubber-based options. Solvent-based tapes use organic solvents to create strong adhesion, making them suitable for demanding applications like package sealing and bundling. They offer excellent temperature and moisture resistance. Conversely, rubber-based tapes use a synthetic rubber adhesive, providing quick initial tack and excellent conformability, ideal for general-purpose uses. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, balancing adhesion strengths and application settings.</p></p>
