<p><strong>The Laparoscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Flexible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laparoscope market is primarily categorized into two types: rigid and flexible laparoscopes. Rigid laparoscopes, known for their straight, fixed structure, are commonly used in surgical procedures like cholecystectomies and appendectomies, providing high-quality imaging and precision. Flexible laparoscopes, on the other hand, feature a bendable design, enabling access to difficult-to-reach areas within the body, making them suitable for complex surgeries such as gastrointestinal procedures. Both types play crucial roles in minimally invasive surgeries, enhancing patient recovery and outcomes.</p></p>
