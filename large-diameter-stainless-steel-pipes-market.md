<p><p>The large diameter stainless steel pipes market includes two primary types: welded and seamless stainless steel pipes. Welded stainless steel pipes are manufactured by welding flat steel plates or strips together, offering advantages like flexibility in size and cost-effectiveness for large-scale projects. Seamless stainless steel pipes, on the other hand, are produced without joints or seams, providing superior strength and durability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types cater to various industries, including construction, oil and gas, and chemical processing.</p></p>
