<p><p>The light vehicle clutches market includes various types such as electromagnetic, friction, and others. Electromagnetic clutches use magnetic fields to engage and disengage, offering precise control and smooth operation. Friction clutches, the most common type, rely on friction plates to transmit power, providing durability and high torque capacity. Other types may include hydraulic or centrifugal clutches, catering to specific applications. Each type serves distinct needs, balancing performance, efficiency, and cost for light vehicles like cars and motorcycles.</p></p>
