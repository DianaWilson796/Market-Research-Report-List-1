<p><p>Mask market types, including N95, N100, P95, P100, and R95, refer to different respirator classifications based on filtration efficiency and resistance to airborne particles. N95 and N100 masks filter at least 95% and 99.97% of particles, respectively, and are not oil-resistant. P95 and P100 masks also filter 95% and 99.97% of particles but are oil-resistant, making them suitable for various environments. R95 masks filter at least 95% of particles and are somewhat resistant to oil, providing versatility in specific settings.</p></p>
