<p><ul><li>Drylaid</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Wetlaid</li><li>Spunlaid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical & Hygiene PP Non-Woven Fabric market encompasses four primary types: Drylaid, Airlaid, Wetlaid, and Spunlaid. </p><p>Drylaid involves entangling fibers through a mechanical process, creating a durable fabric ideal for medical applications. Airlaid utilizes high-velocity air to disperse fibers, producing a soft product suitable for hygiene items. Wetlaid involves a water-based process that creates a robust fabric from fibers, often used in wipes and surgical garments. Spunlaid combines spinning and laying fibers for a lightweight, strong material commonly found in protective apparel.</p></p>
