<p><p>The microwave radio market includes several types: Hybrid Microwave Radio combines both TDM (Time Division Multiplexing) and packet technology, offering flexibility for diverse applications. Packet Microwave Radio focuses on transmitting data packets, making it ideal for modern IP-based networks. TDM Microwave Radio, on the other hand, is designed for traditional circuit-switched networks, efficiently handling voice and video traffic. Each type caters to specific communication needs, driving advancements in wireless connectivity and supporting evolving network infrastructures.</p></p>
