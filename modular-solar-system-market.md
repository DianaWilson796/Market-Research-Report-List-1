<p><p>The modular solar system market encompasses two primary types: grid-tie and off-grid solar solutions. Grid-tie solar systems connect to the utility grid, allowing users to generate electricity, consume it, and sell excess back to the grid, promoting energy efficiency and reducing costs. In contrast, off-grid solar systems operate independently, utilizing battery storage to provide power in remote areas without access to the grid. Both systems support renewable energy adoption, catering to different energy needs and preferences.</p></p>
