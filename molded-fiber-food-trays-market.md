<p><strong>The Molded Fiber Food Trays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type-1 Thick-Wall</li><li>Type-2 Transfer</li><li>Type-3 Thermoformed (Thin-wall)</li><li>Type-4 Processed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molded fiber food trays are categorized into four main types based on their manufacturing processes and applications. Type-1, or Thick-Wall trays, are robust and suitable for heavy items. Type-2, Transfer trays, feature a lighter design, optimized for efficient production and cost-effectiveness. Type-3, Thermoformed (Thin-wall) trays, offer flexibility and are ideal for lightweight packaging needs. Finally, Type-4, Processed trays, are tailored for specialized applications, enhancing sustainability and reducing environmental impact through innovative material use and design.</p></p>
