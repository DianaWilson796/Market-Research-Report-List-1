<p><strong>The MRI Diagnostic Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open MRI</li><li>Closed MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI diagnostic imaging market comprises two primary types: open MRI and closed MRI. Open MRI features a wider, more open design, allowing easier patient access and a less claustrophobic experience, making it suitable for those with anxiety or mobility issues. In contrast, closed MRI utilizes a narrow cylindrical magnet, providing higher-resolution images and faster scans. While closed MRI remains the standard for detailed imaging, open MRI is gaining popularity for its comfort and accessibility, catering to varied patient needs.</p></p>
