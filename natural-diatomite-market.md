<p><p>The natural diatomite market consists of various types, primarily categorized into anhydrous substance and flux calcined forms. Anhydrous diatomite is a high-purity, silica-rich material used for its absorbent properties, commonly applied in filtration, agriculture, and construction. Flux calcined diatomite undergoes high-temperature treatment to enhance its thermal stability and reactivity, making it suitable for specialized applications like metal casting, ceramics, and industrial filtration. Both types serve diverse industries, driven by the demand for eco-friendly and efficient materials.</p></p>
