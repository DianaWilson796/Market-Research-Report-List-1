<p><p>The Near-infrared Spectroscopy (NIRS) equipment market comprises several types, primarily including FT-NIR Spectroscopy and Dispersive NIR Spectroscopy. FT-NIR, or Fourier Transform NIR, offers rapid, high-resolution analysis, making it ideal for complex sample characterization. Dispersive NIR employs optical elements to separate wavelengths, ensuring high specificity for targeted measurements. Other technologies, such as Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTF) and filter-based systems, provide alternative methods for spectroscopy, catering to different application needs and enhancing the versatility of NIRS in various fields.</p></p>
