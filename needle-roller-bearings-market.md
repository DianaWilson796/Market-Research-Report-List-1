<p><p>The needle roller bearings market is primarily segmented into single-row and double-row bearings. Single-row needle roller bearings consist of a single row of rollers, providing high load capacity in a compact design, ideal for limited space applications. Double-row needle roller bearings feature two parallel rows of rollers, enhancing load distribution and stability, making them suitable for heavier loads and higher torque transmission. Both types cater to various industrial sectors, optimizing performance and efficiency in machinery and equipment.</p></p>
