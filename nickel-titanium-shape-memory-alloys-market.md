<p><p>Nickel-Titanium Shape Memory Alloys (NiTi SMAs) can be categorized into two main market types based on temperature phases: High Temperature Austenite Phase and Low Temperature Martensite Phase. The High Temperature Austenite Phase operates effectively at elevated temperatures, exhibiting enhanced mechanical properties, ideal for applications in medical devices and aerospace. Conversely, the Low Temperature Martensite Phase functions at lower temperatures, providing greater flexibility and deformation ability, making it suitable for diverse applications in actuators and robotics. Both phases leverage unique thermal-responsive behaviors.</p></p>
