<p><ul><li>Ferroniobium</li><li>Niobium Oxide</li><li>Niobium Metal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The niobium market can be categorized into three main types: ferroniobium, niobium oxide, and niobium metal. Ferroniobium is an iron-niobium alloy primarily used in steelmaking to enhance strength and resistance to corrosion. Niobium oxide, produced through the oxidation of niobium, is utilized in ceramics and electronics for its capacitance properties. Niobium metal, known for its high melting point and superconducting abilities, is essential in aerospace and medical applications. Each segment caters to distinct industrial needs, driving market demand.</p></p>
