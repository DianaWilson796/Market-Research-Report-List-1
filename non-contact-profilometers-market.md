<p><strong>The Non-Contact Profilometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>White Light Interference</li><li>Confocal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact profilometers are advanced measurement tools used to assess surface topography without physical contact. The market primarily features two key types: white light interference and confocal profiling. White light interference utilizes broad-spectrum illumination to create precise height measurements through interference patterns, suitable for various materials. In contrast, confocal profilometry employs focused laser beams and pinholes to achieve high-resolution depth measurements, allowing for meticulous analysis of complex surfaces. Both techniques are vital for quality control across industries like aerospace, electronics, and manufacturing.</p></p>
