<p><p>The non-reactive adhesives market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Water-based Adhesives** utilize water as a solvent, offering lower toxicity and easy cleanup. </p><p>2. **Solvent-based Adhesives** rely on organic solvents for their adhesive properties, providing strong bonds but often with higher VOC emissions. </p><p>3. **Hot Melt Adhesives** are thermoplastic materials that solidify upon cooling, known for quick bonding and versatility. </p><p>4. **Pressure Sensitive Adhesives** bond upon application of light pressure, ideal for labels and tapes, allowing repositionability and ease of use.</p></p>
