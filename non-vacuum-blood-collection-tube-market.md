<p><p>The non-vacuum blood collection tube market includes various types designed for specific laboratory analyses. Serum separating tubes facilitate the separation of serum from blood cells, making them ideal for biochemical tests. EDTA tubes contain the anticoagulant ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid, preventing blood coagulation for hematological studies. Plasma separation tubes function similarly but are optimized for plasma collection after centrifugation. Other tubes may include specific additives for unique tests, catering to diverse diagnostic requirements in healthcare and research settings.</p></p>
