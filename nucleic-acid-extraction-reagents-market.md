<p><p>The nucleic acid extraction reagents market comprises various types essential for isolating DNA and RNA. Proteinase K is utilized for protein digestion, enhancing nucleic acid purity. NaCl aids in the precipitation of nucleic acids, while CTAB/NaCl solutions facilitate the extraction of DNA from plant tissue. Chloroform/Isoamyl Alcohol (24:1) and Phenol/Chloroform/Isoamyl Alcohol (25:24:1) are organic solvents used for phase separation and purification. Isopropanol and ethanol are critical for precipitating nucleic acids, ensuring high-quality samples for downstream applications.</p></p>
