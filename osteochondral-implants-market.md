<p><ul><li>Allograft</li><li>Screw & Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The osteochondral implants market encompasses various types, including allografts, screws, and plates. Allografts are tissue grafts sourced from donors, providing a natural scaffold for cartilage and bone repair. Screws are surgical devices used to stabilize and fixate osteochondral grafts during the healing process, ensuring proper alignment. Plates serve a similar function, offering structural support and facilitating integration with the surrounding bone and cartilage. Collectively, these innovations aim to enhance joint repair and restore mobility in patients with osteochondral defects.</p></p>
