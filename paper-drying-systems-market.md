<p><p>The paper drying systems market includes various technologies tailored for efficient moisture removal from paper products. Steam heated cylinders utilize steam to transfer heat and dry sheets efficiently. Multi-cylinder dryers optimize space and energy use by employing multiple heated cylinders for continuous drying. Single-tier dryers provide a compact solution, ideal for limited space operations. Flakt dryers use a combination of thermal and mechanical processes, enhancing heat transfer and drying speed. Each type caters to specific production needs and efficiency requirements in the paper industry.</p></p>
