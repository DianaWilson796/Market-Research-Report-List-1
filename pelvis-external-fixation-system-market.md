<p><p>The Pelvis External Fixation System Market is divided into two main types: tubular and circular. Tubular systems consist of rigid rods and pins, providing a straightforward method for stabilizing pelvic fractures. Their ease of application makes them popular in emergency settings. In contrast, circular external fixators offer a more versatile and adjustable approach, allowing for precise control over limb alignment and tension. This adaptability makes circular fixes ideal for complex fractures and deformity correction, enhancing overall patient outcomes.</p></p>
