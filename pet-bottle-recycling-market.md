<p><strong>The PET Bottle Recycling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET bottle recycling market consists of two primary types: chemical and mechanical recycling. Mechanical recycling involves grinding used PET bottles into flakes, cleaning them, and then extruding them into new products, focusing on energy efficiency and simplicity. In contrast, chemical recycling breaks down PET into its original monomers, allowing for the creation of virgin-quality materials. This method is advantageous for contaminated or mixed plastics, providing a sustainable solution for recycling in a circular economy.</p></p>
