<p><ul><li>Conventional Manometry</li><li>High-Resolution Manometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharyngeal manometry testing assesses swallowing function and pharyngeal pressure dynamics. The market is primarily divided into two types: conventional manometry and high-resolution manometry. Conventional manometry utilizes fewer sensors and provides basic pressure profiles, suitable for standard evaluations. In contrast, high-resolution manometry employs a greater number of closely spaced sensors, offering detailed pressure mapping and improved diagnostic accuracy. This advanced technology enhances the understanding of dysphagia and other swallowing disorders, driving its increasing adoption in clinical practice.</p></p>
