<p><p>Phosphor is a substance that exhibits luminescence when exposed to radiation, such as ultraviolet light or electron beams. It is widely used in applications like LED lighting, display technologies, cathode ray tubes, and fluorescent lamps. Phosphors are critical in converting energy into visible light, making them essential in industries such as electronics, automotive, and healthcare.</p><p>The Phosphor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period. The shift toward sustainable and eco-friendly lighting systems has further boosted the market. Additionally, the growing use of phosphors in medical imaging and diagnostic equipment has expanded their application scope.</p><p>Recent trends in the Phosphor Market include the development of rare-earth-free phosphors to reduce dependency on critical raw materials and the emergence of quantum dot phosphors for enhanced color performance in displays.
