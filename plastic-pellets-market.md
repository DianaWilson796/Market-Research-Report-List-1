<p><p>The plastic pellets market comprises various types of polymers, each used for different applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and mainly used in packaging. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is strong and recyclable, common in bottles. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is durable, often used for containers. PE (Polyethylene) covers both LDPE and HDPE. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile, used in construction. PP (Polypropylene) is resistant and lightweight, ideal for automotive parts. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is robust, often found in electronics. Other polymers include various specialty plastics.</p></p>
