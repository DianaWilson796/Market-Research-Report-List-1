<p><p>The Polymer Prefilled Syringe market includes various types of materials used for syringe manufacturing. Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP) is known for its excellent clarity, barrier properties, and compatibility with a wide range of pharmaceutical formulations, making it ideal for biologics. Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC) combines properties of both olefins and offers enhanced strength and lower moisture permeability. Other materials include traditional plastics and glass, which may be used based on specific application needs, cost, and compatibility with drug formulations.</p></p>
