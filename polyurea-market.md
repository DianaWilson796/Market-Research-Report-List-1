<p><p>The polyurea market is primarily divided into two types: pure polyurea and half polyurea. Pure polyurea consists of high-performance coatings that cure quickly, providing superior durability and resistance to chemicals and weather. This makes it ideal for applications like waterproofing and industrial flooring. Half polyurea, on the other hand, combines polyurea components with other materials, offering a more cost-effective solution while sacrificing some performance attributes. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, and protective coating industries.</p></p>
