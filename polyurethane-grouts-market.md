<p><ul><li>Waterborne Polyurethane Grouts</li><li>Solventborne Polyurethane Grouts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane grouts market is primarily divided into two types: waterborne and solventborne polyurethane grouts. Waterborne polyurethane grouts are environmentally friendly, featuring low volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and are commonly used for sealants and coatings in construction and repair. Solventborne polyurethane grouts, on the other hand, contain organic solvents, offering superior adhesion and durability for various applications, including industrial and commercial settings. The choice between these types depends on performance requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
