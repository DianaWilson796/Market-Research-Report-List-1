<p><p>The Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome (POTS) treatment market encompasses primary and secondary forms of the condition. Primary POTS is often idiopathic, requiring treatments focused on lifestyle modifications, increased fluid and salt intake, and medications like beta-blockers or fludrocortisone to manage symptoms. Secondary POTS results from underlying conditions such as diabetes or autoimmune disorders and is treated by addressing the primary disease alongside POTS-specific interventions. Both categories aim to enhance patient quality of life through tailored therapeutic approaches.</p></p>
