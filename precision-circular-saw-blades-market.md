<p><p>Precision circular saw blades are categorized primarily into carbide and diamond types. Carbide blades, made from tungsten carbide, are known for their durability and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for cutting wood, plastic, and softer metals. Diamond blades, on the other hand, feature a diamond-infused edge that provides superior hardness and longevity, enabling them to cut through tough materials like concrete, stone, and ceramic. Both types cater to specific applications, offering users a range of cutting precision and efficiency.</p></p>
