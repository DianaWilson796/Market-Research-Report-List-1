<p><p>The Progesterone Test Kit market can be categorized into two main types: Luminescence Immunoassay and Time-Resolved Analysis. Luminescence Immunoassay utilizes light-emitting reactions to detect progesterone levels in samples, providing rapid and sensitive results. Time-Resolved Analysis, on the other hand, measures the time-dependent fluorescence signal of tagged markers, enhancing sensitivity and accuracy in progesterone quantification. Both methods are crucial for assessing hormonal levels in clinical settings, aiding in fertility treatments and monitoring menstrual cycles.</p></p>
