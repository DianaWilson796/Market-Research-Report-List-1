<p><p>The PVC/PVG solid woven conveyor belt market consists of two primary types: PVC conveyor belts and PVG conveyor belts. In contrast, PVG (polyvinyl chloride and rubber) conveyor belts have a more robust composite structure, providing enhanced strength and fire resistance, suitable for heavy-duty applications such as mining and bulk material handling.</p></p>
