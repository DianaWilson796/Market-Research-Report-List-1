<p><p>The radiant heating panels market comprises two main types: electric radiation panels and infrared radiation panels. Electric radiation panels use electrical energy to directly heat a space by warm surfaces, ensuring even heat distribution. Infrared radiation panels emit infrared waves that warm objects and people in the room instead of the air, providing instant comfort. Both types are energy-efficient, with applications in residential, commercial, and industrial settings, catering to varying heating needs and preferences across different climates.</p></p>
