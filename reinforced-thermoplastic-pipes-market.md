<p><p>The reinforced thermoplastic pipes (RTP) market is divided into two main types: non-metallic RTP and metallic RTP. Non-metallic RTP, primarily made from polymers reinforced with high-strength materials, offers benefits like corrosion resistance and flexibility, making them suitable for various applications in industries such as oil and gas. On the other hand, metallic RTP combines thermoplastics with metal components, providing enhanced strength and durability for high-pressure environments. Both types cater to specific needs in fluid transport and industrial applications.</p></p>
