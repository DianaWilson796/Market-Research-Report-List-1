<p><strong>The Resin Cements Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light-Cured Resin Cements</li><li>Dual-Cured Resin Cements</li><li>Auto-Cured Resin Cements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin cements market is segmented into three main types: light-cured resin cements, dual-cured resin cements, and auto-cured resin cements. Light-cured resin cements harden upon exposure to a specific light source, offering precise control during application. Dual-cured resin cements combine light activation and self-curing properties, making them versatile for various clinical situations. Auto-cured resin cements rely solely on chemical reactions for hardening, providing a straightforward application without the need for additional light sources, suitable for areas with limited access to light.</p></p>
