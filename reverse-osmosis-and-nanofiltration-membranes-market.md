<p><p>Reverse Osmosis (RO) and Nanofiltration (NF) membranes are advanced filtration technologies widely used in water treatment and desalination processes. RO membranes effectively remove a wide range of contaminants, including salts, organic compounds, and microorganisms, making them essential for purifying drinking water and wastewater. NF membranes, on the other hand, operate at lower pressures and primarily remove divalent ions and larger organic molecules, making them suitable for applications like softening water and separating specific compounds.</p><p>The market for RO and NF membranes is experiencing robust growth due to increasing demand for clean water, stringent regulations on wastewater treatment, and rising industrial needs for efficient separation technologies.
