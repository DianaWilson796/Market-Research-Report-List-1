<p><p>The rotary arm stretch wrapper market is categorized into three main types: manual, semiautomatic, and automatic stretch wrappers. Manual stretch wrappers require operator intervention for wrapping, making them suitable for low-volume operations. Semiautomatic stretch wrappers offer a balance, where the machine handles most of the wrapping while the operator assists with pallet loading and unloading. Automatic stretch wrappers fully automate the wrapping process, enhancing efficiency and consistency, and are ideal for high-volume packaging environments that demand speed and precision.</p></p>
