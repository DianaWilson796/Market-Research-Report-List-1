<p><p>The rubber suspension bushes market is primarily categorized into two types: natural rubber suspension bushes and synthetic rubber suspension bushes. Natural rubber bushes are made from organic latex, offering high elasticity and resilience, making them ideal for absorbing shocks and vibrations in vehicles. In contrast, synthetic rubber bushes are composed of engineered polymers, providing enhanced durability and resistance to environmental factors, like oil and heat. Both types serve critical roles in vehicle suspension systems, improving ride quality and handling performance.</p></p>
