<p><p>The self-adhesive labels market includes various printing techniques, primarily flexographic and letterpress printing. Flexographic printing utilizes flexible relief plates to produce high-speed, high-quality prints on a variety of substrates, making it ideal for large-scale production. In contrast, letterpress printing involves directly applying ink to raised surfaces and is known for its tactile quality and vintage appeal. Both methods cater to diverse applications, from packaging to consumer goods, meeting specific branding and labeling needs in the market.</p></p>
