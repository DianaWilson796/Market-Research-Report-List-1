<p><p>The self-expandable metal stent market is categorized into three main types: esophageal stents, gastroduodenal stents, and colon stents. Esophageal stents are designed to alleviate blockages in the esophagus, improving swallowing. Gastroduodenal stents target obstructions in the stomach and the first part of the intestine, enhancing gastric drainage. Colon stents are used to manage strictures in the colon, facilitating bowel movement. Each type enhances patient quality of life by providing minimally invasive solutions to gastrointestinal issues.</p></p>
