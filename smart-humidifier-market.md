<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Humidifier</li><li>Evaporative Humidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart humidifier market includes various types, primarily ultrasonic and evaporative humidifiers. Ultrasonic humidifiers use high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist, providing quiet operation and precise humidity control. In contrast, evaporative humidifiers rely on a fan to blow air through a wet wick or filter, naturally adding moisture to the air as it evaporates. Both types offer smart functionalities, such as app connectivity and automatic humidity adjustments, catering to consumers seeking efficient climate control solutions.</p></p>
