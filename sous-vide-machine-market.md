<p><strong>The Sous Vide Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immersion Types</li><li>Water Bath Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sous vide machine market is primarily categorized into two types: immersion circulators and water bath machines. Immersion circulators are portable devices that heat and circulate water in a separate container, offering precise temperature control, making them ideal for home cooks and professionals alike. Water bath sous vide machines are integrated units that come with a built-in tank, providing a more convenient all-in-one solution. Both types cater to varying user preferences and cooking needs while ensuring consistent results.</p></p>
