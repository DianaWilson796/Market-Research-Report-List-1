<p><p>The soybean oil-based lubricants market includes three key production methods: </p><p>1. **Hydrogenated Method** involves converting soybean oil into a more stable form through hydrogenation, enhancing its viscosity and thermal stability for various applications.</p><p>2. **Epoxidation Method** creates epoxidized soybean oil, resulting in lubricants with improved oxidative stability and low-temperature performance.</p><p>3. **Transesterification Method** transforms soybean oil into fatty acid methyl esters, producing biodiesel and bio-lubricants with favorable properties for eco-friendly applications. Each method offers distinct benefits for diverse lubricant formulations.</p></p>
