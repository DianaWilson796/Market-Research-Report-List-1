<p><ul><li>Non-Vacuum</li><li>Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel lunch container market is divided into two main types: non-vacuum and vacuum containers. Non-vacuum containers are designed for everyday use, offering lightweight, durable options that are typically more affordable. They provide basic insulation but may not keep food hot or cold for long periods. In contrast, vacuum containers feature a double-walled design that creates a vacuum seal, ensuring superior temperature retention and freshness for several hours, making them ideal for more serious meal prep and storage needs.</p></p>
