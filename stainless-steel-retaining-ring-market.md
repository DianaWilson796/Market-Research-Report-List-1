<p><ul><li>Internal Retaining Rings</li><li>External Retaining Rings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel retaining ring market can be divided into two main types: internal retaining rings and external retaining rings. Internal retaining rings fit into a groove on the inside of a bore, securing components within a housing. Conversely, external retaining rings are designed to fit onto a shaft or around a bore, providing a barrier to hold components in place. Both types offer durability and resistance to corrosion, making them essential for various industrial applications.</p></p>
