<p><ul><li>Integral Boiler</li><li>Remote Boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sterilizer steam generators are categorized into two main types: integral boilers and remote boilers. Integral boilers are compact units integrated directly into the sterilizer, providing steam on-demand within the same system. This design simplifies installation and maintenance. In contrast, remote boilers are standalone units that produce steam independently and distribute it to multiple sterilizers. This allows for flexibility in operation and can support larger facilities with higher steam demands, enhancing efficiency and ensuring consistent sterilization processes across various equipment.</p></p>
