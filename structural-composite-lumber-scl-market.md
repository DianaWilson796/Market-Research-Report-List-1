<p><p>Structural Composite Lumber (SCL) encompasses engineered wood products designed for strength and stability. Laminated Veneer Lumber (LVL) is made from thin wood veneers glued together, offering high load capacity and uniformity. Laminated Strand Lumber (LSL) is produced from strands of wood bonded with adhesive, ideal for beams and headers. Parallel Strand Lumber (PSL) consists of long strands aligned in the same direction, providing exceptional strength for heavy-duty applications. Oriented Strand Lumber (OSL) is made from oriented wood strands, suitable for various structural applications.</p></p>
