<p><p>The Super-Junction MOSFET market is primarily segmented into two types: Multiple-Epitaxy and Deep-Trench. Multiple-Epitaxy refers to the technique where multiple layers of semiconductor materials are deposited to enhance operational efficiency and performance, making it suitable for high-voltage applications. In contrast, Deep-Trench technology involves etching deep trenches into the silicon substrate, allowing for improved electric field distribution and reduced on-resistance. Both types are crucial for advancing power electronics in applications such as power converters and electric vehicles.</p></p>
