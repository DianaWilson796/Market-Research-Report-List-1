<p><p>The surgical suction instruments market encompasses various tools essential for maintaining a clear surgical field. Retractors hold back tissues, providing access to the surgical site, while skin hooks facilitate the lifting of skin for better visibility. Needle holders securely grasp needles during suturing. Chisels are utilized for cutting bone or tissue, and gouges help in shaping or excavating material. Together, these instruments enhance precision and efficiency in surgical procedures, catering to diverse medical specialties and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
