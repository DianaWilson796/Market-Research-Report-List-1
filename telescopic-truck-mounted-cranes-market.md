<p><p>The telescopic truck-mounted cranes market primarily consists of two main types: boom cranes and mobile cranes. Boom cranes feature a fixed boom that extends telescopically, suitable for lifting heavy loads in various construction and industrial applications. Mobile cranes, on the other hand, are designed for easy transportation and flexibility, utilizing a rotating boom mounted on a wheeled chassis. Both types enhance operational efficiency, allowing for precise lifting and placement of materials, making them essential in modern construction and logistics sectors.</p></p>
