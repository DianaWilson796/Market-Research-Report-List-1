<p><p>The thermal disc printers market comprises several types: Direct Thermal Printing, which uses heat-sensitive media to create images without ink; Thermal Retransfer, where images are first printed onto a ribbon and then transferred to the media, offering high-quality results; and Dye-sublimation Printing, which involves heating dye to create vibrant, durable images on the substrate.
