<p><p>The thermally modified wood market consists of three primary types: Thermo-S, Thermo-D, and Other. Thermo-S is characterized by a lower temperature treatment, enhancing wood's stability and moisture resistance, making it ideal for indoor applications. Thermo-D involves a higher temperature treatment, improving durability and weather resistance, suitable for outdoor uses. The "Other" category includes specialized treatments and products that may combine various thermal modifications or target niche markets, expanding the range of applications for thermally modified wood.</p></p>
