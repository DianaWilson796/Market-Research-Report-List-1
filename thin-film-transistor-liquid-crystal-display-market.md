<p><p>The Thin-Film Transistor-Liquid Crystal Display (TFT-LCD) market is categorized into three main types: Twisted Nematic (TN), Vertical Alignment (VA), and In-Plane Switching (IPS). TN panels are known for their fast response times and are often used in budget monitors, but they have limited viewing angles and color accuracy. VA panels offer better contrast and deeper blacks, making them suitable for multimedia. IPS panels excel in color reproduction and wide viewing angles, making them ideal for professional applications and high-end displays.</p></p>
