<p><p>The torque converter market for commercial vehicles features two primary types: single-stage and multistage torque converters. Single-stage torque converters utilize a single set of components to transfer power, providing simplicity and efficiency for lighter applications. In contrast, multistage torque converters incorporate multiple sets of turbine stages, enhancing torque multiplication and improving performance in heavier-duty vehicles. This allows for better adaptability to varying load conditions, making multistage options ideal for demanding commercial applications that require higher power and efficiency.</p></p>
