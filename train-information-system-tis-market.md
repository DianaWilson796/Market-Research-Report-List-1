<p><p>Train Information System (TIS) is an integrated platform for real-time train monitoring and communication, enhancing operational efficiency and passenger safety. The market presents various topology types: </p><p>1. **Ring Type**: Nodes connect in a circular manner, allowing data to circulate efficiently but making the entire network vulnerable to a single point of failure.</p><p>2. **Tree Type**: A hierarchical structure where nodes branch out, enabling organized communication flow but possibly leading to scalability issues.</p><p>3. **Star Topology**: All nodes are connected to a central hub, offering robust performance and easy troubleshooting, yet dependent on the central node’s reliability.</p></p>
