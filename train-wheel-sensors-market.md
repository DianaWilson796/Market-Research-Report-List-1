<p><p>Train wheel sensors are crucial for monitoring wheel conditions and enhancing safety. Single wheel sensors focus on individual wheels, providing precise data on parameters like speed, temperature, and wear, which is essential for early fault detection. In contrast, double wheel sensors monitor two wheels simultaneously, offering a broader perspective on the axle's performance and symmetry. This dual approach aids in more comprehensive diagnostics and can improve maintenance strategies, ultimately ensuring smoother operations and reducing downtime in rail networks.</p></p>
