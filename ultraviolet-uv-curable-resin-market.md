<p><ul><li>Monomers</li><li>Oligomers</li><li>Photoinitiators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) Curable Resin market consists of three primary components: monomers, oligomers, and photoinitiators. Monomers are small molecules that polymerize upon UV exposure, forming a solid structure. Oligomers are larger, partially polymerized molecules that enhance the properties and performance of the final resin. Photoinitiators are essential for initiating the curing process; they absorb UV light and generate free radicals or cations, triggering polymerization. Together, these components create versatile resins used in coatings, adhesives, and printing applications.</p></p>
