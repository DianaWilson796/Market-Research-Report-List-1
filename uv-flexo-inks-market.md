<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV flexo inks market comprises two primary types: water-based and solvent-based inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly, featuring low volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them suitable for eco-conscious applications. They provide excellent adhesion and vibrant colors but may have longer drying times. Solvent-based inks, on the other hand, offer quick drying and strong adhesion to various substrates, making them popular in industrial applications. However, they contain higher VOC levels, raising environmental concerns. Each type serves distinct printing needs in various industries.</p></p>
