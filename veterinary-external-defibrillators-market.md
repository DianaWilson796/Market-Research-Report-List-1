<p><p>The veterinary external defibrillator market is categorized into two main types: automatic and semiautomated defibrillators. Automatic defibrillators deliver shocks without the need for user intervention, making them user-friendly and suitable for emergencies where immediate action is critical. In contrast, semiautomated defibrillators require some input from the operator, such as pressing a button to administer a shock, allowing for a balance between clinical oversight and ease of use. Both types play essential roles in treating cardiac emergencies in animals.</p></p>
