<p><p>The Veterinary Stereotactic Radiosurgery System market includes three main types: Gamma Knife, Proton Beam Radiotherapy (PBRT), and CyberKnife. Gamma Knife utilizes precise gamma radiation for targeted tumor treatment in small animals, ideal for conditions requiring minimal invasiveness. PBRT employs protons to deliver targeted radiation, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. CyberKnife combines robotic technology with real-time imaging for guiding radiation to tumors in various animal sizes, offering flexibility and precision in treatment. Each system caters to specific clinical needs in veterinary oncology.</p></p>
