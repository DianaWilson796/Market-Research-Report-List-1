<p><ul><li>Wheel Trenchers</li><li>Chain Trenchers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Walk-behind trenchers are essential for digging narrow trenches for utilities, irrigation, and landscaping. The market consists of two primary types: wheel trenchers and chain trenchers. Wheel trenchers use a rotating wheel with sharp blades to efficiently cut through soil, making them ideal for softer grounds and shallow digs. In contrast, chain trenchers feature a continuous chain with sharp teeth that excavates deeper and handles tougher terrains, making them suitable for more demanding projects where precision and depth are crucial.</p></p>
