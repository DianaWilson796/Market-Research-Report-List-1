<p><p>The wearable devices wireless module market includes two main types: communication modules and positioning modules. Communication modules enable data transmission between wearable devices and other devices or networks, facilitating features like notifications, voice calls, and health monitoring. Positioning modules, on the other hand, provide location services, utilizing technologies like GPS and GLONASS to track users' movements and activities. Together, these modules enhance the functionality of wearables, supporting various applications in fitness, health, and lifestyle management.</p></p>
