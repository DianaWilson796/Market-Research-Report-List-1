<p><p>Welding neck flanges are pivotal in piping systems and come in several types based on their face design. Raised Face (RF) flanges have a raised surface for improved sealing and are commonly used in various applications. Flat Face (FF) flanges, lacking a raised surface, are ideal for low-pressure systems. Ring Type Joint (RTJ) flanges feature a groove for metal gaskets, providing enhanced sealing under high pressure and temperature. Each type serves specific industrial needs, ensuring secure connections in piping networks.</p></p>
